Marriage & Family Seminar

In our world today, we face many challenges within our marriages and families. The Westside church of Christ is committed to serving the families of our community. Come join us for a free special weekend seminar as we seek to strengthen our marriages and families together!

Steve Wages is the Director of the Cloverdale Center for Youth & Family Ministry at Faulkner University.

These classes are offered by the Westside church of Christ. No matter what your native language is, you are welcome to improve your conversational English with us! Caring volunteer teachers help with pronunciation and vocabulary. We use the Bible within our courses for vocabulary and discussion.
